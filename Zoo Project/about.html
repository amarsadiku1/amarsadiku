ABOUT US:

Welcome to the Kosovo International Zoo, a sanctuary of biodiversity nestled in the heart of Kosovo. Our zoo is home to a diverse range of exotic and native species, each cared for by our dedicated team of professionals



We are committed to promoting animal conservation and educating visitors about the wonders of wildlife. Our zoo provides an immersive experience, allowing visitors to explore and learn about different habitats and ecosystems. We believe in creating a better future for all living creatures by fostering an appreciation for nature in our visitors. Join us in our mission to protect and preserve wildlife, and experience the natural world like never before.
